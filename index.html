Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our payday loans

What are the requirements to qualify for a loan?

To qualify, you must be at least 18 years old, a U.S. citizen or permanent resident, have a steady source of income, and an active checking account.

How quickly can I get the money?

If approved, funds are typically deposited into your account within one business day. In some cases, same-day funding may be available.

Will you check my credit score?

We may perform a soft credit check which won't affect your credit score. We focus more on your current ability to repay the loan.

What if I have bad credit?

We consider applications from all credit backgrounds. A less-than-perfect credit score doesn't automatically disqualify you.

Are there any fees to apply?

No, there are absolutely no fees to apply for a loan. You only pay interest and fees if you accept and receive a loan.
